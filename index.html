About

I then finally moved from a Physics department to work in Oxford Materials in the group of Mauro Pasta for then starting my indipendent research career as a EPSRC postdoctoral fellow first, and as a Glasstone Research Fellow after in the Physical and Theoretical Chemistry Lab at University of Oxford. I am also a Junior Research Fellow in The Queen's College and a Stipendiary Lecturer in Brasenose College.
