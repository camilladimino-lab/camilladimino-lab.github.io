The Liquids Database

The Liquids Database provides experimental and curated data for liquid systems. The database is organized into two sections:

Pure Liquids

Data for individual liquid compounds, including thermophysical and transport properties.

Go to Pure Liquids

Binary Mixtures

Data for two-component liquid systems, including mixture properties and phase-equilibrium information.

Go to Binary Mixtures
